Stephen Mackinder

Mackinder Hair has re-established itself as a salon in Copenhagen with an unequivocal Sassoon presence. Along with Stephen, who had worked for 32 years Sassoons in London and Los Angeles, he now joined by Lela and Zee [PIA?].

Lela achieved her cosmology licence in Los Angels at the Vidal Sassoon School and has a remarkable talent in cutting hair the Sassoon way, carefree to wear and suitably beautiful.

Zee has spent more than two years at Vidal Sassoon Schools in London and Los Angeles. He has spent time on various courses, hair cutting and colour, has a passion for creating breathtaking looks.

Along with Lela and Zee, Pia who has now been at Mackinder Hair for six years has achieved her Master Colour at Wella, and she combines her wonderful colour techniques with great haircuts.

To back all this up Tina Mackinder once a teacher at the Vidal Sassoon School in London joins this exciting team for dressing courses at Mackinder Hair. If you want a great haircut or colour, there is no better place than Mackinder Hair.

We believe technique, knowledge and creative thinking are the foundations of our hairdressing

We believe that our strength in technical excellence gives us the freedom to create hair that works for our clients and inspires other hairdressers

We want our clients to stand apart, to wear beautiful hair that is cut to fit only them.

We want to share our foundations with hair professionals from around the world, interested in acquiring the technical expertise to release their creative potential

We want to excel in the delivery of every one of our services.

Mackinder Hair was established in the summer of 2006 to bring premium quality hairdressing services and education to Copenhagen and the rest of Scandinavia.
